Our Mission

We provide broad range of buy and selling in the industry of textile, cosmetics, gift items, electronic and electric items etc.., retail and wholesale. We serve for the successful development of small business and medium entrepreneurial industry as well as we assist entrepreneurial sector, through the growth stages and toward business development, ensuring regulatory compliance along the way. With over 38 years of experience we have built up a wealth of knowledge in our field and we are operating with six branches with fame in the textile industry in the North Central Province, and Central Province to be noted. We are renowned for our extensive service. All of our services are here to satisfy the customer needs and wants with a variety of choices where our motto is “everything is for your choice”. Our head office is located at no. 32, Main Street, Kekirawa, and we are working with an expert team of business consultants who are very motivated and have made us building up a solid reputation with our customers due to our professional and personal service.
